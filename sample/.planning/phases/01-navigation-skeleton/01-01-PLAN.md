# Plan 01-01: Map the Office and Drive to the Kitchen

## Goal

The robot builds a floor map and can navigate from its dock to the kitchen
without human help.

## Tasks

1. [x] Scan the floor with lidar and persist the occupancy grid
2. [x] Implement A* route planning over the grid
3. [x] Drive the route with obstacle re-planning

## Acceptance

- Robot reaches the kitchen from the dock in under 90 seconds
- No collisions across 10 consecutive runs
